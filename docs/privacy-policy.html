Privacy Policy

Tally Night — Last updated: March 24, 2026

Tally Night is an entertainment app only. BAC estimates are approximate and should never be used to determine fitness to drive or operate machinery. This app does not provide health, medical, or legal advice. Always drink responsibly.

What this app does

Tally Night estimates your blood alcohol content (BAC) based on the drinks you log, your weight, and gender. It is designed as a fun social tool for use between friends and is not a medical device or diagnostic tool.

Data stored on your device

The following data is stored locally on your device using encrypted storage. It never leaves your device unless you explicitly enable data sharing for the Groups feature:

  • Your name, gender, and weight
  • Drink history (type, volume, ABV%, timestamps)
  • Water intake history
  • Stomach status
  • Custom drink presets

Data shared with our server

If you choose to enable the Groups feature, the following data is sent to our server:

  • Your display name
  • Your current BAC level (a single number)
  • Drink counts by category (e.g. 2 beers, 1 wine)

The following data is never sent to our server, regardless of your sharing settings:

  • Gender and weight
  • Drink timestamps, volumes, or ABV percentages
  • Stomach status
  • Water intake

Your BAC is a single number. Without knowing your weight, gender, or when you drank, it is not possible to reverse-engineer personal details from a BAC value and drink counts alone.

Data retention

Server-side data is automatically deleted when:

  • You leave all groups or disable data sharing (immediate deletion)
  • A group expires (6, 12, 24, or 48 hours after creation, as set by the group creator)
  • Your account has been inactive for more than 30 days (automatic cleanup)

Local data remains on your device until you manually clear it through the app or uninstall the app.

Third-party services

This app uses Convex (convex.dev) as a backend service for the Groups feature. No data is shared with any other third parties. We do not use analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Your rights

You can view or delete your local data at any time through the app. You can delete all server-side data by disabling data sharing in the Data & Privacy settings. Since the app does not require an account or email address, there is no personal data retained beyond what is stored on your device and optionally on our server while the Groups feature is active.

Age requirement

This app is intended for users who are of legal drinking age in their jurisdiction. By using this app, you confirm that you meet this requirement. The app includes an age confirmation step during setup.

Children's privacy

This app is not intended for use by anyone under the legal drinking age. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.

Changes to this policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy or your data, please open an issue on our GitHub repository or contact us through the app store listing.
